Copying Characterizations
To copy characterizations, open the nanoparticle characterization form that displays the characterization. See Navigation Tree—Physical Characterizations or Navigation Tree—In Vitro Characterizations. The lower portion of the form is the section where you specify to copy the annotation data for the open particle.
The characterization files and/or derived data for this nanoparticle can be copied to other particles from the same vendor.
To copy characterizations, follow these steps in the Copy section of the page:
1.
Select one or more particles to which the copied files and summary information is to be transferred. This option copies files and summary information to the selected particle.
2.
To copy derived data, as well, you must also select the Also Copy Derived Data? hypertext link.
3.
Click Submit. The new characterization is added to the particle(s) you seleced with an auto-generated title.
