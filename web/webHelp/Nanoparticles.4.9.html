Performing a Remote Nanoparticle Search
When you launch a nanoparticle sample search, you can specify at the bottom of the search form nanoparticle data service sites you want to search. You can search for publicly available nanoparticles from all active caNanoLab data services connected to the caBIG™ grid (caGrid™). Not only are searches conducted on caGrid, but this feature publishes the caNanoLab domain model on caGrid for the benefit of other users of caNanoLab.
For more information about caGrid, see https://cabig.nci.nih.gov/workspaces/Architecture/caGrid.
See Searching Existing Nanoparticles for more information.
