Toxicity Subcategory
Toxicity refers to the potential of nanoparticles to be harmful to cell health or viabilty. Because toxicity is ascertained under laboratory conditions, it is one of the In Vitro Characterization categories of caNanoLab.
Toxicity subcategories include the following:
•
Toxicity Subcategory: Cytoxicity
•
Toxicity Subcategory: Enzyme Induction
•
Toxicity Subcategory: Immunotoxcity
•
Toxicity Subcategory: Oxidative Stress
